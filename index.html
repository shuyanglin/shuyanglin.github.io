About

I am a designer. I mostly make new processes. I am currently at the Central Government of Taiwan, making new processes for civic participation and policy making.

I am a strong believer that the government can do great on confronting global issues, if we help them. I was invited to join Taiwan Digital Minister’s Office, and co-founded PDIS in 2016. We created an innovation lab in the central government, and have since then worked on open government, social innovation and youth council.

With PDIS, I help manage the design of cross-ministerial policy making process, work on digital regulatory reforms with grassroot civic tech communities, and support operation of social innovation lab and launch youth incubation hub.

On the side, I help consult my friends in industries to grow their user-friendly projects with our planet Earth in mind. In 2020, I teamed up with my partner and created 1522 Collective to practice people-planet approach through projects around food, democracy, works and education.

For a more extensive look into my work experience, you can read my CV.

When not at work, I enjoy jogging along river banks, cooking perfect soft boiled eggs and browsing Brackeys Unity tutorials.
